Hi there, I'm Khoi Minh Truong!

I am currently pursuing a Master of Professional Studies in Applied Economics and Management at Cornell University, with a strong background in Economics, Business, and Computer Science. My academic journey has taken me from Wabash College, where I graduated with a Bachelor of Arts in Economics and earned several academic honors, to the vibrant academic community here at Cornell.

My passion lies in the intersection of economics, data analysis, and strategic decision-making. Throughout my academic and professional experiences, I have honed my skills in quantitative analysis and coding, with proficiency in Python, R, and various analytical tools. I am driven by a desire to apply these skills to real-world challenges in the financial sector and contribute to data-driven solutions.

As a graduate student, I have engaged in rigorous coursework that has further deepened my understanding of economics, econometrics, and computational methods. I have also had the privilege of working on award-winning research projects that explore the dynamics of infectious disease spread and the impact of financial crises on income inequality.

Outside of my academic pursuits, I have actively contributed to various organizations and projects, from editing newsletters to conducting market research and managing cultural outreach initiatives. I am a firm believer in the importance of interdisciplinary collaboration and the value of diverse experiences.

In my spare time, you can find me exploring the beauty of the Ithaca landscape, cycling, and scuba diving. I am also passionate about the stage and have played a crucial role in ensuring the success of college productions. I once even performed in Devised Theatre!
